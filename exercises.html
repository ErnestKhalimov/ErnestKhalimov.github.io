Here are the exercises you should do.


These eleven exercises are pretty much all you need to build muscle in the gym.

Bench Press

Bench greatly developes middle chest,front delts and triceps every begginer should definetly do this.

Incline Bench Press

Incline bench press developes upper chest making your whole chest look thicker and better.

Shoulder Press

Shoulder press is crucial for shoulder development and making your physique look much better. You can optionally switch dumbells for machine but free weights fatigue the muscle more effectively.

T-Bar Row

T-Bar rows are amazing for upper back and rear delt develepment..

Barbell Row

Barbell Rows are amazing for developing the Lats and middle back.

Barbell Squat

Barbell Squats are a must if you want to develop your lower body especially the quads.

Chest Fly

Chest flies are amazing for a chest pump and really focusing on the pectorial muscle

Leg Press

The leg press might get a bad rep in the fitness community, but i think it is a wonderful exercises for begginers at the gym. It provides stability and good control of the weight which fatigues the muscle.

Lateral Raises

Absolute must for big shoulders and getting that v-taper physicue.

Tricep Pushdown

At the end of your workouts where you do bench, i would highly suggest doing these to absolutely fatigue the triceps to a failure.

Bicep Curls

Lastly for big arms you should do biceps curls after doing any kind of rowing movement.
