Here ive listed 3 optimal splits for beginners


These 3 workout splits are pretty much the only one you need when you start. You should pick the one that fits your timetable. Click the link below the splits to learn more.



"P P L"

PPL stands for Push,Pull,Legs in essence it is a 3 to 6 days split which focuses on concentrating on a smaller area of the body in a particular day.



PPL

Upper/Lower


Upper/Lower is the greatest split for beginners in my opinion since it allows you to train 4 days a week optimally while hitting your upper and lower muscles twice a week.



Upper/Lower

Full Body


Fullbody is also great for beginners since you hit the entire body at once.However doing all muscles in a single workout can be tiring so i would reccomend pairing it with another split eg.Upper/Lower and doing both of the at the same time.
